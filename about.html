About Bluegrass Handyman Services

Your trusted neighborhood handyman since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Bluegrass Handyman Services began with a simple mission: to provide Central Kentucky homeowners with reliable, skilled, and honest handyman services.


Owner and lead technician Mike Thompson started his career working in residential construction before realizing there was a growing need for versatile, trustworthy handyman services throughout the Lexington area. What began as a one-man operation has grown into a well-respected local business serving six communities.
